# Working Agreement

---

## Communication
- We will use **Slack** for quick updates, discussions, and informal communication.
- Important decisions, updates, and technical discussions will be documented through **GitHub Issues and Pull Requests (PRs).**
- Urgent issues should be flagged in Slack and followed up with an issue in GitHub.
- All members are expected to check **Slack and GitHub notifications** regularly.

---

## Meetings
- **Weekly meetings** will be held **every Friday at 5 PM** to discuss progress, blockers, and next steps.
- Additional ad-hoc meetings may be scheduled as needed.
- Any member unable to attend a meeting must inform the group **at least 24 hours in advance** and review the meeting notes.
- **Meeting minutes** will be documented in GitHub for reference.

---

## Decision Making
- Decisions will be made **collaboratively**, and all members are encouraged to share their input.
- If consensus cannot be reached, we will use **majority voting** to finalize decisions.
- Critical decisions affecting **project scope or deadlines** must be discussed in a meeting before implementation.

---

## Code Review
- All **code changes** must be submitted via **Pull Requests (PRs).**
- Each PR must be reviewed and approved by **at least one other team member** before merging into the main branch.
- PRs should be **properly documented**, with clear descriptions of changes.
- Reviewers must check for **code quality, adherence to standards, and proper documentation.**

---

## Conflict Resolution
- We will address conflicts **directly and respectfully**, focusing on the issue, not the person.
- If necessary, a **mediator from the group** will help resolve conflicts.
- If a conflict remains unresolved, the issue will be **escalated to the instructor** for guidance.

---

## Accountability
- Each member is responsible for their **assigned tasks** and must communicate any challenges they encounter.
- Members should update the status of their tasks in the **project management tool (GitHub Issues).**
- If a task is delayed or blocked, the member must **notify the team** and seek help if needed.
- Every team member is expected to **contribute equally** and **respect deadlines.**

---

## Documentation & Version Control
- **Documentation** must be updated when significant changes are made.
- The **main branch** must always be stable and deployable.
- Feature branches should follow a naming convention (e.g., `feature-<task-name>` or `bugfix-<issue-id>`).
- Merge conflicts must be resolved before merging into `main`.

---

## Review and Adaptation
- This **Working Agreement** will be reviewed at the **end of each sprint.**
- If necessary, **adjustments** will be made to improve collaboration and efficiency.
